What is the weather like? 天氣點樣？
Mary:
Hi Timmy, how are you today?
嗨，蒂米，你今日點啊？
Timmy:
Hi Mary! I'm good, thank you. How about you?
嗨，瑪麗！我好，多謝。你呢？
Mary:
I'm good too, thanks. What did you do over the weekend?
我都好，多謝。你個週末做咗乜嘢呀？
Timmy:
I went to the park with my family. It was so much fun!
我同家人去公園。真係好開心啊！
Mary:
Wow, that sounds great! What did you do there?
哇，聽起嚟好正啊！喺度你做咗乜嘢呀？
Timmy:
We played soccer and had a picnic. What about you? What did you do?
我哋踢波同野餐。你呢？你做咗乜嘢？
Mary:
I went to the beach with my parents. It was awesome!
我同父母去海灘。太好啦！
Timmy:
Really? What did you do at the beach?
真係咩？你喺海灘上做咗乜嘢？
Mary:
We built sandcastles and swam in the ocean. It was so refreshing!
我哋築沙堡，喺海里游水。真係令人耳目一新！
Timmy:
That sounds like a lot of fun! What was the weather like at the beach?
聽起嚟好有趣！海灘嘅天氣點樣？
Mary:
It was hot and sunny! Perfect beach weather.
天氣炎熱，陽光明媚！完美嘅海灘天氣。
Timmy:
Yeah, it's been really hot lately, hasn't it?
係啊，呢排真係好熱，唔系吗？
Mary:
Yeah, but I like it. I love summer!
係呀，但我中意。我愛夏天！
Timmy:
Me too! Hey, do you want to play soccer with me after school today?
我都係！嘿，你今日放學後想唔想同我一齊踢波呀？
Mary:
Sure! That sounds like fun. Let's meet at the playground at 4 o'clock.
得啊！聽起嚟好有趣。喺4點喺操場見啦。
Timmy:
Okay, see you then!
好嘅，到時見！
What color is your bag?
Sally:
Hi Tommy! How was your weekend?
Tommy:
Hi Sally! It was great. I went to the park with my family. What about you?
Sally:
I had fun too! I went to the zoo with my mom and dad.
Tommy:
Wow, that sounds exciting! Did you see any monkeys?
Sally:
Yes, lots of them! They were swinging from tree to tree.
Tommy:
Cool! Hey, what color is your bag?
Sally:
My school bag is red. What about yours?
Tommy:
Mine is blue. We have different colors!
Sally:
Yeah, that's right! It's fun to have different colors. We can easily find our bags at school.
Tommy:
Definitely! Well, I better go now. See you tomorrow, Sally!
Sally:
Bye, Tommy! See you tomorrow!
What time is it?
Jack:
Hey Sarah, do you want to play some games?
Sarah:
Sure, Jack! But first, what time is it?
Jack:
It's ten thirty.
Sarah:
Oh, we still have plenty of time to play!
Jack:
Yeah, let's play hide and seek. I'll count first, and you hide.
Sarah:
Okay, I'll find a good hiding spot.
Jack:
Ready or not, here I come!
Sarah:
(giggles) You'll never find me!
Jack:
Found you! You were behind the couch.
Sarah:
You're too good at this game, Jack.
Jack:
Now it's your turn to count. I'll hide this time.
Sarah:
Alright, I'll count to twenty. One, two, three...
Jack:
(whispering) Where should I hide? Oh, I know!
Sarah:
...eighteen, nineteen, twenty! Ready or not, here I come!
Jack:
(from behind the curtains) You'll never find me here!
Sarah:
Found you! You were hiding behind the curtains.
Jack:
Aw, you're too good at this game too, Sarah.
Sarah:
Let's play another game. How about tag?
Jack:
Sounds fun! You're it!
Sarah:
(chasing Jack) I'm going to catch you!
Jack:
(laughing) You'll never catch me!
Sarah:
Gotcha! You're tagged!
Jack:
Aw, you're too fast, Sarah!
Sarah:
Tag, you're it!
Jack:
Okay, let's play again!
Sarah:
Sure, let's go!
Jack:
But first, what time is it?
Sarah:
It is ten thirty.
Jack:
Great, we still have time for more games!
Sarah:
Yeah, let's play until it's time for lunch!
Jack:
Sounds like a plan!
